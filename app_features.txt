**Hospital Management System App Features (Detailed):**

**Core Modules:**

*   **Dashboard:**
    *   Displays key metrics like total patients, appointments, and revenue.
    *   Shows a list of today's appointments.
    *   Provides quick access to other modules.

*   **Patient Management:**
    *   Add, view, edit, and delete patient records.
    *   Search for patients by name or ID.
    *   View patient details, including personal information, medical history, and appointments.

*   **Employee Management:**
    *   Add, view, edit, and delete employee records.
    *   Search for employees by name or ID.
    *   Assign roles to employees (admin, doctor, etc.).

*   **Appointments:**
    *   Book appointments for patients with specific doctors.
    *   View a list of all appointments.
    *   Filter appointments by date, doctor, or patient.
    *   Update the status of appointments (scheduled, completed, canceled).

*   **Medical Records:**
    *   Create and manage patient medical records.
    *   Record diagnosis, treatment, and prescriptions.
    *   View a patient's complete medical history.

*   **Pharmacy Management:**
    *   Manage the hospital's pharmacy.
    *   Add, view, edit, and delete medicines.
    *   Manage medicine categories.
    *   Track medicine stock levels.
    *   Receive low stock alerts via SMS.
    *   Dispense medicines for prescriptions.

*   **Billing & Accounting:**
    *   Generate bills for patients.
    *   Track payments and outstanding balances.
    *   View a list of all transactions.
    *   Manage the hospital's finances.

**Patient Portal:**

*   **Patient Dashboard:**
    *   Provides patients with an overview of their upcoming appointments, recent medical records, and outstanding bills.
    *   Quick access to other portal features.

*   **Book Appointments:**
    *   Allows patients to book appointments with doctors.
    *   View a list of available doctors and their schedules.

*   **View Medical Records:**
    *   Allows patients to view their own medical records, including diagnosis, treatment, and prescriptions.

*   **View Billing Information:**
    *   Allows patients to view their own billing history, including invoices and payment status.

*   **Patient Profile:**
    *   Allows patients to view and edit their own profile information, including personal details and contact information.

**Authentication:**

*   **Staff Login:**
    *   Secure login for staff (admins and doctors).
*   **Patient Login:**
    *   Secure login for patients to access the patient portal.
*   **Patient Registration:**
    *   Allows new patients to create an account.
    *   Strict email validation to ensure only valid email addresses are used.
*   **Forgot Password:**
    *   Allows patients to reset their password via a link sent to their email.
*   **Social Login (Google):**
    *   Allows patients to log in using their Google account.

**Other Features:**

*   **Dark Mode:**
    *   The app has a dark mode theme for a better user experience in low-light environments.
*   **Responsive Design:**
    *   The app is designed to be responsive and work on different screen sizes, from mobile phones to desktops.
*   **SMS Notifications:**
    *   The app sends SMS notifications for important events, such as:
        *   Appointment bookings and confirmations.
        *   Low stock alerts for the pharmacy.
*   **Android App:**
    *   The app is packaged as a native Android app using Capacitor, allowing it to be installed on Android devices.
